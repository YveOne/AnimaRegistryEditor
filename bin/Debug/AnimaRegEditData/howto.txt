- Click 'Select Gamesave' to open a slot

When editing while playing:

- Switch to game, press ESC and select 'Continue from previous...'
  to load edited Gamesave values.

After opening a Gamesave:

- Edited values wont get saved directly. Click on "Save" or "Save as"
  to apply all changes done to opened Gameslot.

- When you switch from Game to Editor make sure to click on "reload"
  to load the newest Gamesave values.
